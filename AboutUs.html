Our Story

For over a decade, Vintage Chic Furniture has been transforming homes with our exquisite dining tables that blend timeless elegance with modern craftsmanship.

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by master carpenter Narges Pech, Vintage Chic Furniture began as a small workshop with a big vision: to create dining tables that weren't just furniture, but heirlooms that would be passed down through generations.

What started as a passion project in a garage has blossomed into a beloved brand known for its commitment to quality, sustainability, and timeless design. Each table in our collection is crafted with the same attention to detail and love that went into that very first piece.

Today, while we've grown beyond that single workshop, we still maintain the same artisanal approach to furniture making. Every table is hand-finished by our skilled craftsmen using techniques perfected over decades.
